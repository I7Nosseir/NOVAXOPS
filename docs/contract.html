The Developer has independently designed, built, and deployed a custom software platform (the "Platform") currently used by the Agency to manage its client accounts, content pipeline, approvals, publishing, reporting, and AI-assisted workflows. This agreement formalises the commercial arrangement between both parties.

1 — Ownership

The Platform, including all source code, architecture, integrations, design, and future modifications, is and remains the exclusive intellectual property of the Developer. This agreement does not transfer ownership of the Platform to the Agency under any circumstances.

2 — License Grant

The Developer grants the Agency a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Platform for its internal business operations for as long as this agreement is active and payments are current. The Agency may not sublicense, resell, or share access to the Platform with third parties.

3 — Fees

One-Time Build Fee — for Platform delivery (payable upon signing)   EGP
Monthly Retainer — maintenance, updates, and new features   EGP / month
Retainer Start Date

Monthly retainer is due on the 1st of each calendar month. Payments more than 14 days overdue suspend the Developer's service obligations until the outstanding amount is settled.

4 — What the Monthly Retainer Covers

Bug fixes and technical maintenance  •  New features and enhancements  •  Third-party integration updates  •  Deployment and hosting support  •  Reasonable technical support requests from the Agency.

5 — Other Clients & Commercial Use

The Developer retains the right to offer the Platform or a derivative product to other clients or as a commercial product, provided the Agency's private data and configurations are never shared. If the Agency wishes to be the sole licensee, an exclusivity clause must be agreed separately in writing at an additional cost.

6 — Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination the Agency's license to use the Platform ends. The Agency may export its data within the notice period. The Developer is not obligated to hand over source code at any point.
